About Buns Studio

Discover the story behind the art.

My Journey

What makes my work truly unique is the philosophy behind it: I believe that everyone is born an artist. Each of us has a spark of creativity within, waiting to be nurtured. My own story is proof that art can emerge from the most unexpected circumstances. I started selling goods and products at 14 to support myself, learning life’s lessons in resilience, resourcefulness, and connecting with people. These experiences now shape the way I create—capturing not just the image, but the essence of every subject I draw.

Inspiration also flows from my father, an artist whose legacy reminds me that creativity runs deep. Yet, my art is my own—a blend of life’s challenges, hard-earned skills, and the belief that everyone has the potential to create something beautiful.

Every portrait I craft reflects this journey, celebrating the artist in all of us. Through my work, I aim to inspire others to recognize their own creativity and see the beauty in their unique stories.

Mission

To create meaningful and expressive charcoal and graphite portraits that bring stories to life, inspire emotion, and connect with people on a personal level.

Vision

To inspire others to embrace their creativity and see the beauty in life’s challenges. To make art accessible and impactful, serving as a testament to resilience and creativity.
